MARK BRENNER


VOCALS, BASS, GUITAR, DOUBLE BASS, SITAR, SANTOOR, KEYBOARDS

Mark was born in England and lived there until 1995, when he moved to France.
Previous bands include: Moving Fingers, Paparazzi, Go Ask Alice and The Persuaders.
Influenced by a wide range of musical styles and artists. Equipment: G&L L-2500 5-string bass, Fender 60's Classic Stratocaster, Rickenbacker 4001 V63 bass, Höfner 500/1 Vintage '62 violin bass, German acoustic double bass (named Bertha), Sanjay Rikhi Ram Grand Pro Tun sitars, Takamine New Yorker electro-acoustic guitar and Lag ukuleles. Mark endorses Shadow Nanoflex pickups for the sitar and the double bass.

THOMAS DROUART


KEYBOARDS, PROGRAMMING, BASS AND BACKING VOCALS

An experienced musician (previous bands include Tribal Jam, Post Image, Melting Pot),
Thomas has his feet and musical preferences firmly planted in everything that grooves! A writer and arranger in his own right, he’s also a keen sportsman (marathon man).
Equipment: Korg M3 and Triton Pro, Moog Phatty, Roland FP5, Kirtzweil, Apple Macintosh G4, Yamaha Motif,
"Tomtom Special" customized Fender Jazz bass, Takamine electro- acoustic bass.



ANTONY BREYER


DRUMS, PERCUSSION, BACKING VOCALS

Antony is the new boy in the band. He joins us with lots of experience (he's played with some great bands), some great ideas... and he even sings!



TOM SMITH


GUITAR AND BACKING VOCALS

Born and bred in Bristol, England, Tom moved to France aged 11. He comes from a musical family (his dad
is a professional singer-songwriter). Tom has inherited the capacity to come up with vocal harmonies very easily. Equipment: Fender Stratocaster and Telecaster, Gibson SG, Takamine electro-acoustic guitar.




JEAN-CHRISTOPHE JACQUES


SAXOPHONE

Jean-Christophe has been playing with us for over a year now... and he's proved himself to be the saxophone king! J-C is proud to be a Keilwerth saxophones endorsee.
